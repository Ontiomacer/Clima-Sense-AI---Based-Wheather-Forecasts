## For New Users

### 1. First Visit

When you visit ClimaSense AI for the first time:

1. **Landing Page**: You'll see a professional landing page showcasing features
2. **Explore**: Scroll through to learn about the platform
3. **Sign Up**: Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up" button

### 2. Create Account

Two ways to sign up:

**Option A: Email/Password**
1. Click "Get Started"
2. Enter your email address
3. Create a password
4. Verify your email (check inbox)
5. You're in!

**Option B: Social Login**
1. Click "Get Started"
2. Choose "Continue with Google" or "Continue with GitHub"
3. Authorize the connection
4. You're in!

### 3. Access Dashboard

After signing up:
- Automatically redirected to `/dashboard`
- See real-time climate data
- Access all features
